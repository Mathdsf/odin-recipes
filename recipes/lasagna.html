Lasagna

"This is a faster, and less expensive lasagna."

ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to teste
  • 1 (16 ounce)jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint rocotta cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 a 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and galic and simmer 5 minutes
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, cheddar and riccota; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
